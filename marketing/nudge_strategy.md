# 🎯 Behavioral Marketing Strategy — Crowd Yield via Nudge Theory

**Objective:** Redistribute discretionary guest flow from a hyper-crowded zone
(**Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge**) to an under-utilized, high-margin zone
(**EPCOT World Showcase**) — lifting guest satisfaction *and* in-park spend at once.

The play is a gamified quest — **"The Galactic Explorer Challenge"** — layered onto
the Disney App. Each stage maps to a distinct behavioral lever.

---

## 🟦 Step 1 — The Pre-emptive Nudge *(morning, before crowds form)*

**Levers:** Fresh-start effect · Goal-gradient (endowed progress) · Anchoring.
Seed the quest before guests anchor on Galaxy's Edge.

> ✨ *"Good morning, Explorer! Your **Hidden Mickey Quest** has begun in EPCOT's
> World Showcase. Visit 3 pavilions before noon to unlock your exclusive
> **Spaceship Earth Badge**. Two stamps are already waiting — your adventure
> starts now!"*

*Endowed progress ("two stamps already waiting") is one of the strongest known
drivers of task completion.*

---

## 🟨 Step 2 — The Real-Time Redirection *(triggered when Galaxy's Edge hits the crowd-yield cap)*

**Levers:** Social proof (positively reframed) · Scarcity · Loss aversion.

> 🚀 *"The galaxy is buzzing right now! Fellow adventurers are discovering shorter
> waits and a limited-time treat over in EPCOT. Tap to claim your **Wonder Pass**
> before it vanishes — only **500 left today**."*

*Choice architecture: we never say "Galaxy's Edge is full." We make the
alternative feel like the smarter, scarcer prize.*

---

## 🟥 Step 3 — The Reward Fulfillment *(on arrival at EPCOT)*

**Levers:** Variable reward · Loss-leader nudge · Goal-gradient.

> 🎉 *"You did it, Explorer! Badge unlocked. Show this at any World Showcase
> pavilion for a complimentary festival treat — and you're just **1 stamp away**
> from the Golden Mouse Ears reward. Keep the magic going!"*

*The free treat lands the guest inside a Food & Wine booth, where the average
party adds paid items; "1 stamp away" sustains forward movement and spend.*

---

## 💰 ROI Projection — In-Park Spending (modeled, peak day, single park)

| Funnel stage | Assumption | Guests |

| Peak-day attendance | — | 65,000 |

| Incremental spend / redirected guest | **+$16.50** | — |

**Daily incremental in-park spend ≈ 1,459 × $16.50 ≈ $24,100**

| Scenario | Conversion | Peak days/yr | Annual incremental spend |
|---|---|---|---|
| 🔴 Conservative | 14% | 75 | ≈ **$1.15M** |

> **Note:** Figures are modeled projections built on the stated, transparent
> assumptions above, for portfolio-demonstration purposes.

**Secondary (un-monetized) benefit:** redirecting ~1,400 guests/day measurably
lowers Galaxy's Edge wait times, lifting satisfaction (NPS) — a long-run driver of
repeat-visit lifetime value that compounds beyond the direct spend figure.
